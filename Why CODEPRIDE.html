Here’s how we test

Every applicant goes through extensive vetting, which means you’re guaranteed quality work from day 1.

01

Self-assessment quiz

We get thousands of applicants each month. The first step our applicants take is to complete a quiz where they answer questions about their background and professional experience.

03

Code test

Promising applicants are tasked with a challenging code test, where they are using the language and framework they want to work with at CODEPRIDE.

05

Final review

Our team performs a full review of each stage of the applicant’s vetting process to ensure consistency.

02

Technical quiz and screening interview

Applicants take a technical quiz, which helps us have a more fact-based discussion in our screening interview. Our recruiters assess the applicant’s professional experience, English proficiency and soft skills.

04

Technical interview

Our engineering managers perform an extensive evaluation of the applicant's technical and problem-solving skills during a 1-hour interview.

06

Onboarding & ongoing review

Our community managers chat with our newcomers to give them a warm welcome and discuss practicalities. We also have frequent reviews of all of our developers to ensure high-quality density in our network.
